Edmund's Vehicle Dataset API
Edmund's API played a vital role in helping the team retrieve large amounts of vehicle data from numerous major manufacturers. This data allowed the team to establish unique models for different vehicles and manufacturers. Our hope is to help our users find their own perfect car by helping them understand the complex relationships between different makes and models from only the best manufacturers.
